CentSense

A quiet nudge, right after you spend.

Real-time budget awareness, one text at a time.

What it does

CentSense watches a single individual's own linked bank and card accounts, and sends that same person a text message right after each purchase — noting how much of their budget for that spending category has been used. At the end of each day, it sends a short summary of that day's transactions.

It's a personal tool, built and used by one person to keep an eye on their own spending — not a product offered to customers or the public.

How messaging is used

  • Messages are sent only to the account owner's own verified phone number.
  • There is exactly one recipient: the person who built and uses the tool.
  • Message volume is low — typically a handful of texts per day, tied directly to real purchases.
  • Sample message: "You've spent 75% of your Eating Out budget this period ($202 / $269)."
